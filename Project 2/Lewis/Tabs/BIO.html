LEWIS WARREN JR. BIO RECORDINGS CONTACT

IN THE SPIRIT
PIANIST. COMPOSER. WORSHIPPER

Lewis Warren Jr. is an award winning and accomplished composer/arranger. He began composing original pieces at the age of five and has been a frequent top prize winner in local, state and international competitions since he was eight. Lewis' gift as a pianist has be described as nothing short of phenomenal.
Lewis has participated in or spoken to a multitude of programs and venues such as:

THE MORTON H. MEYERSON FAMILY TZEDAKA FOUNDATION

THE VAN CLIBURN AWAKENINGS PROGRAM

SOWER OF SEEDS

THE EMPTY BOWLS FOUNDATION AT WHICITA FALLS

VISION AFRICA

REAL CHOICES

TEDXSMU AND FIRST PRESBYTERIAN DALLA

More intimate venues include:
PRIVATE HOMES, BANQUETS, SCHOOLS, WEDDINGS, AND CHURCHES

Lewis is studying piano performance at the New England Conservatory under Victor Rosenbaum.
He loved doing things different and being the first to try something out. Eventually design became more than a passion to him.
